About Us

Welcome to Station Hill Baptist Church. We meet every Sunday to worship God and preach the Gospel, actively seeking to serve the community. Our church is committed to making a positive impact on people of all ages.

Our Services

  • Youth Work: Our dedicated youth worker serves the young people of the town.
  • Toddler & Mums Groups: Held twice a week.
  • Lunches for the Elderly: We provide lunches for elderly folk once a month.
  • Special Elderly Service: A monthly service dedicated to the elderly community.
  • Community Support: Our premises are regularly used as a Child Contact Centre and by other charities.
